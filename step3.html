Step 3: Install CPU

The CPU is one of the most critical components of your build, and proper installation ensures optimal performance and prevents damage.

Start by removing the motherboard from its packaging and placing it on top of its box or a static-free surface.

You’ll find the CPU socket located on the upper central part of the motherboard. Follow these steps to install the CPU:

  • If the socket has a latch, release it by gently pushing down on the lever and pulling it outwards. For sockets with a cover, lift the cover upwards carefully.
  • Locate the alignment markings or notches on both the CPU and the socket. These will guide you to correctly orient the CPU.
  • Gently place the CPU into the socket, ensuring it is perfectly aligned and seated without applying force.
  • Push the lever back down to secure the CPU in place. Ensure the CPU is firmly locked but avoid over-tightening or excessive pressure.

Once the CPU is installed securely, you’re ready to proceed to Step 4.
