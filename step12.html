Step 12: Optimize PC

Now that your PC is built and ready to go, it's time to optimize it for the best performance.

1. Optimization Tips:

There are plenty of tutorials available online that guide you through optimizing your PC. However, be cautious! Some guides may suggest disabling certain services or making system changes that can be difficult to reverse.

Important: If you're going to follow any tutorial, we strongly recommend creating a Restore Point beforehand. This will allow you to revert any unwanted changes if necessary.

2. Recommended YouTube Channel:

One of the most reputable sources for Windows optimization is the YouTube channel KhorviTech. They offer effective tips for improving your system's performance, and many users have reported great results.
