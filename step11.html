Step 11: Download Drivers

Now that your operating system is installed, itâ€™s time to download the necessary drivers to ensure all your hardware components work properly.

1. Visit Manufacturer Websites:

Go to the websites of your PC parts' manufacturers (motherboard, GPU, storage, etc.) to download the latest drivers. This will ensure that your components are running with the most up-to-date software.

2. Windows Update:

If you're using Windows, you can easily download additional drivers via Windows Update. To do this:

  • Go to Settings > Update & Security > Windows Update.
  • Click Check for Updates to download any available drivers.

3. Restart Your PC:

After all drivers are installed, restart your PC to complete the process and apply any updates.

Once you're done with this, move on to Step 12!
