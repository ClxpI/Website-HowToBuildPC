Step 2: Prepare Your Workspace

Creating an organized and safe workspace is crucial for building your PC efficiently and without stress. Start by selecting a tidy, well-lit area where you can work comfortably.

Ensure you have all the essential tools at hand, including:

  • A screwdriver (preferably magnetic-tipped),
  • Cable ties for clean cable management,
  • Thermal paste for the CPU, and
  • An anti-static wrist strap to prevent static discharge from damaging your sensitive components.

Having a clutter-free space and the right tools will make the building process smoother and reduce the risk of errors or damage.

Once your workspace is ready, proceed to Step 3.
