Step 10: Download Operating System

Congratulations! You're almost there! Now it's time to get your operating system (OS) up and running.

1. Prepare a USB Drive:

Get a USB pen drive with at least 8GB of storage.

2. Download the OS:

Choose the operating system you want to install on your new PC.

  • Windows: Search for "Download Windows" in your browser and select the version you're looking for. Microsoft's official website will guide you through the process.
  • Linux: If you prefer Linux, visit the official Linux download page to get the version that best suits your needs.

3. Follow the Instructions:

Follow the instructions on the respective website to download the OS to your USB drive.
